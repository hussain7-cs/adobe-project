Frequently asked questions.

Can I make a QR code on my mobile device?

Absolutely. The Adobe Express app is quick and easy to use on smartphones and other mobile devices. Simply open the QR code generator, add your desired link, choose a style and color, download the file, and you're done.

Can I make a QR code for a link?

All QR codes must be connected to a link for them to work properly. Here’s how to create a QR code for a link:


1. Open the QR code generator

2. Enter your URL

3. Tap Create QR Code

4. Customize your QR code

5. Download your QR code

How do I scan a QR code?

Simply open the camera app on your mobile phone, point your camera toward the QR code you want to scan, and tap the QR code to bring it into focus. Once a pop-up banner with the link’s QR code appears, tap it and voila.

How long are QR codes valid for? Do they expire?

Unlike on some QR code generator sites that only offer a free trial, QR codes generated in Adobe Express don’t expire. QR codes are as important as logos; try one on your business card or vCard – it's much easier for someone to scan than remember a mobile phone number or lengthy URLs.
